About SKSSF

Our History & Vision

Founded in 1989, the Sunni Kerala Students Federation (SKSSF) was established with the noble intention of guiding the student community towards moral and educational excellence. For over three decades, we have been at the forefront of social reform, educational initiatives, and community service in Kerala and beyond.

Our Vision: To cultivate a generation of students who excel in modern education while remaining deeply rooted in Islamic values and ethics, contributing positively to the nation's progress.

Our Mission: To provide platforms for students to develop their skills, offer scholarships to the needy, organize awareness campaigns against social evils, and foster a spirit of unity and brotherhood among the youth.

Our Flag & Identity

The Tricolor Flag

The official flag of SKSSF features a tricolor layout representing the key values and identity of the organization:

  • Blue Section (Top-Left): Represents the skies of knowledge, faith, and the light of truth (featuring the crescent and star).
  • White Section (Top-Right): Symbolizes peace, moral purity, and sacred values (featuring the mosque dome and minaret).
  • Green Section (Bottom): Represents social service, growth, youth development, and progress (featuring the white SKSSF text).
